Welcome to Triangle Tweener Fund

The Triangle Tweener Fund strategy is to build an index – smaller investments in a relatively large number of Triangle-based companies. At the current fund size, this translates to 10-15 investments a quarter, or 40-60 a year.

Our primary focus is Tweeners ($1m/yr in revenue and up), but we occasionally invest in early stage companies (Seed and Series A).
